# These are all grammatical examples. The elided positions are indicated by ___.
# Source: Jason Merchant (2016) Ellipsis: A survey of analytical approaches


Lauren can play five instruments, and Mike can play six ___ .
----
Lauren can play five instruments, and Mike can play six instruments.

Beth’s wedding was in Bond Chapel, and Rachel’s ___ was in Rockefeller Chapel.
----
Beth’s wedding was in Bond Chapel, and Rachel’s wedding was in Rockefeller Chapel.

Beth’s nuptials were in Bond Chapel, and Rachel’s ___ were in Rockefeller Chapel.
----
Beth’s nuptials were in Bond Chapel, and Rachel’s nuptials were in Rockefeller Chapel.

Sviatslav is pro-communist and Derzhinsky is anti- ___ .
----
Sviatslav is pro-communist and Derzhinsky is anti-communist.
