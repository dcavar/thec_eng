# These are all grammatical examples. The elided positions are indicated by ___.

Billy ___.
----
Billy has been hiding the truth.
B: Who has been hiding the truth? 
# wikipedia: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ellipsis_(linguistics)

___ This damn crossword.
----
I have been trying to accomplish this damn crossword.
B: What have you been trying to accomplish? 
# wikipedia: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ellipsis_(linguistics)

___ Tomorrow.
----
The circus starts tomorrow.
B: When does the circus start? 
# wikipedia: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ellipsis_(linguistics)

___ Due to the personalities.
---- 
The campaign has been so crazy due to the personalities.
B: Why has the campaign been so crazy?
# wikipedia: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ellipsis_(linguistics)

Yeah, ___ some ___.
----
Yeah, I have some good ideas.
B: Do you have any good ideas?
