# Notes:
# The constructions with gapping are all ungrammatical. The full forms are all grammatical.
# The assumed elided positions are indicated by ___.

Sam said that they spoke German, and Charlene ___ Spanish.
----
Sam said that they spoke German, and Charlene said that they spoke Spanish.

# Note: No gapping across a finite clause boundary.

He ___ beer, and she orders wine.
----
He orders beer, and she orders wine.

I read the story about elves, and you read the story about dwarves.
